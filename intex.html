About Our Village

The Heart of Tradition

Poocholamad is a picturesque village known for its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and warm-hearted people. Nestled between lush green fields and gentle hills, our village offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.

With a history spanning centuries, Poocholamad has preserved its traditional way of life while embracing modern developments. Our village is famous for its annual festivals, local handicrafts, and organic farming practices.

The villagers take great pride in maintaining the cleanliness and ecological balance of our surroundings, making Poocholamad a model for sustainable living.

Village Attractions

Ancient Temple

Our 300-year-old village temple is an architectural marvel and spiritual center, hosting vibrant festivals throughout the year.

Serene Lake

The village lake is a perfect spot for birdwatching and enjoying breathtaking sunsets over calm waters.

Organic Farms

Visit our organic farms to learn about traditional farming methods and taste fresh, chemical-free produce.
