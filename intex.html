Welcome to Manjery

A vibrant city with rich culture, beautiful landscapes, and a thriving community. Discover the charm of Manjery and experience its unique blend of tradition and modernity.

About Manjery

Our History

Manjery has a rich history dating back centuries. Founded in the early 18th century, our city has grown from a small agricultural community to a thriving urban center while maintaining its cultural heritage and traditions.

Culture & Traditions

Manjery is known for its vibrant festivals, traditional crafts, and warm hospitality. Our annual Harvest Festival attracts visitors from across the region, showcasing local music, dance, and culinary delights.

Modern Manjery

Today, Manjery is a perfect blend of tradition and progress. With excellent educational institutions, healthcare facilities, and a growing tech industry, our city offers the best of both worlds - the charm of a close-knit community with the amenities of modern urban living.

Our local government is committed to sustainable development, ensuring that as we grow, we preserve our natural beauty and cultural identity for future generations.

Village Highlights

Historical Landmarks

Explore our ancient temples, colonial-era architecture, and the famous Manjery Clock Tower that has stood for over 200 years.

Culinary Delights

Taste our famous Manjery farsa and other local specialties that have been passed down through generations.

Natural Beauty

Discover the breathtaking Manjery hills and our protected forest reserves with diverse flora and fauna.

Community Spirit

Experience our famous hospitality and community events that bring everyone together throughout the year.

Education Hub

Home to prestigious schools and vocational training centers known for academic excellence.

Economic Growth

A thriving local economy with opportunities in agriculture, handicrafts, and technology.
